{# why #}

Why Am I Doing This?

The 1:1 coaching sessions during my consulting projects feel like the most valuable and effective part of my work. Back in university, I had some important mentors but I wish I had similar guidance once I started out working as a junior designer — and even later in my career!

I also see a lot of companies offer educational budget but few additional opportunities to grow their talent. Since it is hard to hire in tech these days and good leaders with coaching skills are rare, I feel like there is a gap I can fill.

So this is as a test-run to get valuable feedback and eventually turn this into a professional service — to help people in digital product design to grow and develop.
